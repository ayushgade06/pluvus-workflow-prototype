# Drafting Humanization — Sample Replies (Before vs After)

> **What this is:** real `/draft` output rendered through the production LLM stack,
> comparing the **old `v1.x` prompts** (on `main`) against the **new humanized
> `v2.0` prompts** (branch `ayush/drafting-humanization`).
>
> **Stack used:** `LLM_PROVIDER=openrouter` · classify + negotiate = `anthropic/claude-opus-4.8` ·
> **drafting = `deepseek/deepseek-chat-v3.1`** (the model that actually writes these emails).
>
> **Scenario (held constant across every reply):** brand **Stridr** (premium
> trail-running shoes), creator **Maya** (trail running, Instagram). Hybrid deal —
> fixed fee **+ 10% commission**, deliverables **2 IG Reels + 1 IG Story**, timeline
> **live by Aug 15 2026**, payment **Net-30 after content goes live**. Same creator
> messages fed to both versions.

---

## At a glance

| Stage | Before (words) | After (words) | What changed in the copy |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Outreach | — | 120 | prose product paragraph, deal in a sentence |
| Follow-up | — | 57 | short nudge, no re-pitch |
| First offer (r1) | 109 | 79 | prose + direct Q&A instead of a 4-bullet dump |
| Mid-thread counter (r2) | 120 | 86 | **drops the "commission adds up" sales pitch**; states only the changed fee |
| Mid-thread hold (r3) | 104 | 61 | **stops re-dumping all 5 terms**; answers only what was asked |
| Onboarding | 97 | 69 | warm "deal just closed" open instead of a form letter |

**Length trend (after):** 120 → 57 → 79 → 86 → 61 → 69. Emails taper as the thread
progresses instead of padding to a ceiling every turn.

**Guarantees verified on every real draft:** fee stated exactly, commission exactly
10%, exclusivity/timeline/payment answered from real data (never invented), final-offer
finality present, signs as "Stridr" (never "Pluvus").

## Observations & tuning levers (for Phase 3)

1. **The biggest tell is gone.** The "let me explain why this offer is fair / how the
   commission adds up" narration that ran on every counter is removed. This was the
   single strongest "AI generated" signal.
2. **Say-only-what-changed works.** Round 3 no longer re-dumps the full offer; it
   answers the questions asked and confirms the standing number.
3. **DeepSeek still likes short lists** where the prompt permits them (≥3 items, or a
   2-question answer). That's within the new rules (bullets are conditional, not
   banned) and reads fine. If you want *more* prose there, it's a prompt-tuning lever,
   not a bug.
4. **Follow-up "Just wanted to…"** skirts the banned "just following up" opener. Easy
   to tighten in `_VOICE_BLOCK`'s banned-phrase list if it bothers you.

*All numbers, answered questions, fixed-term holds, and finality language were verified
correct on every real draft above.*
